Pockex

Cash register and accounting for exchange operations: rates, trades, clients, and balances in one interface.

Core capabilities

  • Buy/sell deals and cross-exchange flow in one workflow.
  • Cash register visibility and per-currency balance control.
  • Client records, notes, backups, and data transfer tools.
